South India (Highest Demand)

Accounts for 42% of national bus travel. Strong state transport networks in Tamil Nadu (TNSTC), Karnataka (KSRTC), and Andhra Pradesh (APSRTC) ensure reliable connectivity.

West India (Growing Market)

Maharashtra and Gujarat show 35% year-over-year growth. Industrial centers like Mumbai-Pune and Ahmedabad-Surat corridors drive demand.

North India (Emerging Potential)

Delhi NCR and Punjab lead northern states. Government initiatives in Rajasthan and UP improving connectivity.

East India (Developing Infrastructure)

West Bengal dominates eastern region. Kolkata-centric routes most popular, rural connectivity improving.
